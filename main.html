Plot Summary

Inception is a sci-fi thriller directed by Christopher Nolan. The movie follows a skilled thief, Dom Cobb, who is an expert in "extraction," a process of stealing secrets from within the subconscious during dreams. Cobb is offered a chance to erase his criminal record by performing "inception," the planting of an idea into someone's mind, a task thought to be impossible.
