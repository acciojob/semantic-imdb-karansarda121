Plot Summary

"Inception" is a 2010 science fiction heist film directed by Christopher Nolan. It revolves around Dom Cobb, a thief who enters the dreams of others to steal their secrets. Given a chance to clear his criminal record, Cobb must pull off the ultimate challenge: performing "inception," the act of planting an idea in someone's mind.
