Plot Summary

"The Dark Knight" is a 2008 superhero film directed by Christopher Nolan. The film follows Bruce Wayne/Batman as he faces off against his most dangerous adversary, the Joker, who seeks to destroy Gotham City through chaos and anarchy. With help from his allies, Batman must confront the Joker's plans while battling his own ethical dilemmas as Gotham's protector.
